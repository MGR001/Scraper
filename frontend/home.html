Fair questions

The things you'd ask on the call

Is this just scraping?

It reads public pages, public feeds and public forums — the same things you'd read yourself, on a schedule you'd never keep. It respects robots.txt and skips anything gated.

Will it bury me in alerts?

One brief, one time a day, and only when something actually moved. Cosmetic edits and nav changes never reach you.

How long to set up?

Paste one URL per competitor. RIvals finds the sitemap, the feed and the subreddits itself. First brief lands inside the hour.

Does it work with what we use?

Briefs go to email or Slack. Battlecards and comparison matrices export to PDF and slides, so they land where sales already looks.
