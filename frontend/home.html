Fair questions

The things you'd ask on the call

What sites does it monitor?

Any public website or RSS/Atom feed you add. Paste a URL and RIvals finds the sitemap, crawls every linked page, and re-indexes on the schedule you set — from hourly to weekly.

How does the AI analysis work?

Content is chunked and embedded with OpenAI (text-embedding-3-small). The chat uses GPT-4o with RAG — answers are grounded in your competitors' actual published pages, not model training data.

How does change detection work?

Every rescrape is compared to the previous session using content hashes. New pages, removed pages and altered content are surfaced in the Insights tab — nothing falls through the cracks.

What are the GTM tools?

A segment heatmap plots competitors against your ICPs. Positioning teardown reconstructs each rival's core claims. Campaign messaging generates ready-to-use copy angles — all generated on demand from your indexed data.
